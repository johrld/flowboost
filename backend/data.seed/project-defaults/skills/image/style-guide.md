# Image Generation Skill

## Style

- Clean, modern, professional
- Soft natural lighting
- Warm but not oversaturated colors
- Minimal clutter — focus on the subject

## Composition

- Rule of thirds for subject placement
- Negative space for text overlays
- Hero images: landscape 16:9 ratio
- Social images: match platform ratio (1:1 for feed, 9:16 for stories)

## Mood

- Approachable and inviting
- Calm confidence, not high-energy
- Real-world settings over abstract
- People in natural situations (not stock-photo poses)

## What to Avoid

- Overly dramatic lighting or HDR effects
- Cluttered backgrounds
- Text baked into the image (overlays are added later)
- AI artifacts (extra fingers, distorted text)
- Generic stock photo aesthetics

## Hero Image Guidelines

- Must relate directly to the article topic
- Should work without text overlay
- High resolution (at least 1200px wide)
- Subject clearly identifiable at thumbnail size
