# X (Twitter) Post Skill

## Tone & Voice

- Concise and punchy — every word must earn its place
- Opinionated over neutral
- Conversational, not formal
- Wit is welcome, forced humor is not

## Structure (Single Tweet)

- Lead with the strongest point
- No filler words or unnecessary context
- End with a question or bold statement
- 1-2 hashtags max (feels spammy otherwise)

## Thread Format

- Tweet 1: hook that stands alone (must make people click "Show thread")
- Tweets 2-N: one point per tweet, numbered if helpful
- Last tweet: summary + CTA (follow, retweet, reply)
- No "Thread" or thread emoji clichés

## Formatting

- No line breaks within a single tweet (280 chars doesn't allow it)
- Numbers and specifics over vague claims
- Quotes and contrarian takes perform well
- Links in the last tweet or reply (they reduce reach in main tweet)

## Do

- Make bold claims you can defend
- Use numbers: "5 things...", "In 2024, we..."
- Quote-tweet with commentary
- Reply to your own tweets for threads

## Don't

- Start with "Hot take:" (it's a cliché)
- Use more than 2 hashtags
- Thread every thought (some things are one tweet)
- Auto-cross-post from LinkedIn (different audience)
