# Instagram Post Skill

## Tone & Voice

- Visual-first — the image/carousel must stand alone
- Casual, authentic, relatable
- Use storytelling in captions
- Emoji usage: moderate (2-4 per post, context-appropriate)

## Structure

1. **Hook** (first line) — shows in preview before "...more"
2. **Body** (2-4 short paragraphs) — story or value
3. **CTA** (1 line) — save, share, comment, or follow
4. **Hashtags** (below a line break or in first comment)

## Carousel Rules

- 5-10 slides with clear progression
- Slide 1: bold statement or question (the hook)
- Each slide: one key point, large readable text
- Last slide: summary + CTA
- Consistent visual style across slides

## Hashtag Strategy

- Mix of broad (100K+ posts) and niche (<10K posts)
- 10-15 hashtags (sweet spot for reach)
- Place below content or in first comment
- No hashtags in the caption body

## Image Guidance

- High contrast, clean composition
- Text overlays: large, readable, max 5 words
- Consistent color palette with brand
- Faces and people increase engagement

## Do

- Tell a story, even in short form
- Use the "save this for later" CTA
- Create educational carousels
- Show behind-the-scenes content

## Don't

- Post without an image (media is required)
- Use low-resolution or cluttered images
- Write captions longer than 2200 characters
- Use only broad hashtags
