# TikTok Skill

## Tone & Voice

- Conversational and authentic
- Talk like you're explaining to a friend
- Energy: enthusiastic but not forced
- Trend-aware when relevant

## Structure (Video Script)

1. **Hook** (first 3 seconds) — the most important part
2. **Setup** (5-10 seconds) — context
3. **Value** (main content) — the insight or story
4. **Payoff** (last 5 seconds) — conclusion + CTA

## Hook Patterns

- "Did you know that..."
- "Stop scrolling if you..."
- "Here's something nobody tells you about..."
- "POV: you just discovered..."
- Start mid-action (no intro, jump right in)

## Caption Rules

- Short and supplementary (caption supports video, doesn't replace it)
- Include a CTA: "Follow for more", "Save this"
- 3-5 relevant hashtags
- Trending sounds/hashtags when authentic

## Photo Post Rules

- Clean, eye-catching image
- Text overlay with key message
- Carousel: educational content works well
- Caption: more detailed than video captions

## Do

- Get to the point immediately
- Use trending formats when they fit your topic
- Speak directly to camera or use voiceover
- End with a hook for the next video

## Don't

- Start with "Hey guys, welcome back"
- Use corporate language
- Make videos longer than 60 seconds (unless story-driven)
- Ignore comments (engagement drives reach)
