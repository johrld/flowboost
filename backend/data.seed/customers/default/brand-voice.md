# Brand Voice

## Mission

Provide helpful, accurate content that solves real problems for our audience.

## Kernwerte

1. **Clarity** - Simple language, short sentences, no jargon
2. **Accuracy** - Evidence-based claims, cite sources when possible
3. **Practical** - Actionable advice, concrete examples, step-by-step guides

## Zielgruppe

- Professionals seeking practical knowledge
- Beginners looking for clear introductions to topics
- Decision-makers researching solutions

## Tonalität

| Do                          | Don't                      |
| --------------------------- | -------------------------- |
| Warm and approachable       | Cold or lecturing          |
| Direct and clear            | Vague or evasive           |
| Encouraging                 | Pushy or demanding         |
| Professionally casual       | Overly formal or stiff     |

## Sprachregeln

### Ansprache

- Use "you" / "du" (informal)
- Prefer active voice
- Short sentences (max 20 words)
- Short paragraphs (max 4 sentences)

### Bevorzugen

- Concrete examples
- Numbers and facts (with sources)
- Everyday language
- Encouragement over pressure
